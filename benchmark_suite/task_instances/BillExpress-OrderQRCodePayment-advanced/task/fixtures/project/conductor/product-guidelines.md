# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Professional yet approachable**: Maintain a helpful and clear tone suitable for retail and business users.
- **Concise & Direct**: Use short sentences and simple terminology. Avoid overly technical jargon when communicating errors or instructions.
- **Consistent Terminology**: Always refer to the core entities consistently (e.g., "Customer", "Invoice", "Product", "GSTIN").

## Branding
- **Visual Identity**: Clean, modern, and minimalistic.
- **Colors**: Rely on Tailwind CSS default palettes with a focus on trustworthy blues and neutral grays, complemented by distinct semantic colors for alerts (red/yellow/green).
- **Typography**: Utilize system sans-serif fonts for optimal readability and performance.

## UX Principles
- **Speed First**: Prioritize rapid keyboard navigation and quick entry forms since POS users need to operate fast.
- **Error Forgiveness**: Provide clear error messages, undo actions (e.g., restoring canceled invoice stock), and prominent confirmation modals for destructive actions.
- **Responsive Design**: Ensure the dashboard and invoice views are readable on various device sizes, including tablets commonly used at checkout counters.
